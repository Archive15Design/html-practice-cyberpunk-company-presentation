Helix Corporation Research Divisions

  • Neuro-enhancement : Developing new brain-computer interfaces to allow for seamless communication between humans and machines, with a focus on enhancing cognitive performance and sensory capabilities.
  • Genetic Engineering : Using CRISPR technology to develop new ways of modifying human DNA, with the goal of improving disease resistance, extending lifespan, and enhancing physical abilities.
  • Cybernetic Implants : Designing and testing new types of cybernetic implants, such as robotic limbs and enhanced sensory devices, that can integrate seamlessly with the human body and provide new capabilities.
  • Regenerative Medicine : Investigating new ways of regenerating damaged tissue and organs, including the use of stem cells and 3D printing technology, to create new treatments for injuries and diseases.
  • Synthetic Biology : Using synthetic biology to create new forms of life, with the goal of developing new sources of energy, creating new materials, and exploring the limits of what is possible in the field of biotechnology.
